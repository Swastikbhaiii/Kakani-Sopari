Manufacturing Process

At Kakani Sopari, we source our betel nuts from the fertile lands of Karnataka, where each nut is carefully chosen for its quality. Our team brings these premium sopari to Gujarat, where they undergo a meticulous slicing process, preserving freshness and enhancing flavor. The sliced sopari is then expertly packaged in our state-of-the-art facility, ensuring it meets the highest quality standards.

Once packaged, our products are ready for distribution across various markets. We are proud to be recognized for our commitment to quality at every stage, from sourcing and processing to packaging and delivery. Kakani Sopari stands for trust, excellence, and tradition, serving customers with unmatched dedication.
